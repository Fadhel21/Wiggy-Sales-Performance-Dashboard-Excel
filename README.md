# Swiggy-Sales-Performance-Dashboard-Excel

An interactive dashboard displaying Swiggy's sales performance, including Total Sales of $53.01M, Average Rating of 4.34, Avg Order Value of $268.51, Rating Count of 5.59M, and Total Orders of 197.43K. It features monthly, daily, and weekly sales trends, a comparison of Veg vs Non-Veg sales, a state-by-state sales distribution map, and the Top 5 cities with the highest sales to support analysis and decision-making.

## Dashboard Features

**1. Sales Trend Analysis**
- Monthly Sales Trend (Jan – Aug)
- Daily Sales Trend (Sun – Sat)
- Weekly Sales Trend (Week 1 – 36)

**2. Sales by Food Type**
- Comparison of Veg vs Non-Veg sales contribution

**3. Sales by State**
- Geographic distribution map of sales, rating, and orders across India
- Quarterly breakdown (Q1, Q2, Q3) of Sales, Rating, and Orders by state

**4. Top 5 Cities by Sales**
- Ranking of highest-performing cities, including Bengaluru, Lucknow, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and New Delhi

## Filters / Slicers

- **Months** — Filter data by specific month(s)
- **Category** — Filter by food category (e.g., Desi, Chinese, Pasta, Rolls, Sides, etc.)
- **Restaurant Name** — Filter by specific restaurant

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, Slicers, Map Chart, Conditional Formatting)
